About M&K Laundry Services

M&K Laundry Service is a trusted name in garment care, serving homes and businesses across Greater Accra. We specialize in wash & fold, ironing, dry cleaning, stain removal, and pickup & delivery services. Our goal is to make laundry effortless, affordable, and reliable for everyone.

Our Goals

  • Provide consistent, top-tier laundry services across Accra
  • Expand our pickup & delivery network to reach more neighborhoods
  • Maintain eco-friendly practices in cleaning and packaging
  • Build lasting relationships with our customers through trust and care
