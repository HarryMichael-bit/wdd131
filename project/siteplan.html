Site Plan

Site Name

M&K Laundry Services

I chose this name because it clearly represents the business focus on professional Laundry services, while also being simple and easy to remember. The "M&K" adds a presonal branding touch.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the M&K Laundr Services website is to provide customers with convenient accessto our professional laundry and dry-cleaning services. The site will allow users to learn about services, view pricing, schedule pickups and deliveries, and contact us easily. Our goal is to make laundry care simple, reliable,a nd stress-free.

Target Audience

The primary audience for M&K Laundry Services includes busy professionals, students, and families in urban areas who need reliable and affordable laundry care. They value convenience, time-saving solutions, and professional results. Many of them may not have the time or equipment to handle laundry themselves, so they look for services that offer pickup, delivery, and flexible scheduling.

In addition, institutions such as churches, schools, hotels, hospitals, and corporate offices are also part of our target audience. These organizations require bulk laundry services on a regular basis and value efficiency, consistency, and professional quality to meet their operational needs.

Site Goals

  • Provide clear information about our laundry and dry-cleaning services, including pricing and packages.
  • Offer an easy way for customers to schedule laundry pickups and deliveries online.
  • Build trust and credibility by showcasing our reliability, customer service, and professional quality.

Domain Name

A possible domain name for this website is mklaundryservices.com. This name is simple, professional, and easy to remember. It clearly communicates the business focus and would be easy for customers to type and find online.

Scenarios

  • How can I schedule a laundry pickup and delivery?
  • What are the prices for washing, ironing, and dry-cleaning services
  • Do you offer bulk laundry services for institutions like schools or hotels?

Color Scheme

The color scheme for M&K Laundry Services is designed to reflect freshness, cleanliness, and trust. The following colors will be used consistently across the site.

  • Primary Color: #1E90FF (Dodger Blue) - used for headers, buttons, and highlights.
  • Secondary Color: #FFFFFF (White) - used for backgrounds to create a clean, open look.
  • Accent Color #FF0000 (Red) - used for call-to-action buttons and important links.
  • Text Color: #33333 (Dark Gray) - used for body text for readability.

Typography

The typography for M&K Laundry Services is chosen to ensure readability, professionalism, and a modern look:

  • Headings: Montserrat - used for all headings (h1, h2, h3) to create a bold and modern style.
  • Body Text: Roboto - used for paragraphs and general content for easy readabilty.
  • Fallback: Sans-serif - used as a backup font family if the primary fonts are unavailable.
